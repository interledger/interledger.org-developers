Japan has numerous annual events, including national holidays like New Year (Jan 1), major festivals such as Obon (August) and cherry blossom viewing (Hanami, March-April), seasonal celebrations like Tanabata (July), and children's events like Shichi-Go-San (Nov). Major seasonal festivals are also key, such as autumn leaves viewing (Nov-Dec) and winter illumination
